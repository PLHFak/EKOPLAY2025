About EKO

Synthetic Audio Dynamic Reversion Technology

What is EKO?

EKO is a cutting-edge synthetic audio technology that generates realistic, synchronized sound for slow-motion video replay. Using advanced SADR (Synthetic Audio Dynamic Reversion) algorithms, EKO creates dedicated sonic layers that maintain perfect sync at any playback speed.

The Problem: Traditional replay systems can only pitch-correct audio by approximately 15-25% of normal speed. Beyond that, audio becomes unusable, forcing broadcasters to rely on ambient crowd noise or silence during slow-motion replays.
The Solution: EKO generates entirely new audio tracks that are perfectly synchronized with video at any speed—3×, 5×, 10× slower or more—delivering the sonic impact that matches the visual drama.

Key Features

  • Perfect synchronization at any playback speed (1× to 10×+ slower)
  • Sport-specific training models for enhanced realism
  • Isolated audio tracks ready for mixing with commentary and crowd
  • Real-time processing for live broadcast integration
  • Compatible with existing replay systems (EVS, Grass Valley, etc.)
  • Customizable impact intensity and sonic characteristics

Technical Specifications

Slow-Motion Range

1× to 10× (and beyond)

90-100% sport-specific match footage

Use Cases

Sports Broadcasting: Enhance slow-motion replays with realistic impact sounds (boxing, football, basketball, etc.)

Live Events: Maintain audio engagement during slow-motion highlights

Post-Production: Add synthetic audio to archival footage or content without original sound

Training & Analysis: Provide audio-visual feedback for athlete performance review

How It Works

1. Video Analysis: EKO analyzes the video frame-by-frame to detect key events (impacts, movements, etc.)

2. Sonic Generation: Using trained neural networks, EKO generates appropriate sounds for each detected event

3. Synchronization: Audio is precisely aligned with video at the target playback speed

4. Mixing: The isolated EKO track is delivered to audio engineers for blending with commentary and ambient sound

Demo Note

The EKOPLAY demonstration uses a general-purpose model trained on diverse sports footage. For production deployment, EKO models are trained on 90-100% sport-specific match footage, significantly enhancing audio fidelity, impact recenticity, and overall realism.
